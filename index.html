Lokhandwla Foundation School

Freedom Through Education

Lokhandwala Foundation Trust established the co-educational English medium Lokhandwala Foundation School in the year 1992. The School initially started with a small building in the Whispering Palms Housing CHS. Later, it was shifted to its new campus nearby, which has a four-floored building with a well structured playground. The new building also includes a large sized auditorium on the ground floor.The Lokhandwala Foundation School is an English-medium school in Lokhandwala Township, Akurli Road, Kandivali, Mumbai, India. The school offers the ICSE board syllabus for standard X students. The new school building has four floors, a basement, a terrace and a multipurpose basketball court

Campus Life

School Library

Was established in 1992.

This is one of the favourite places for students who have a hobby of reading books reading books. This library is a very big area of shelves filled with books, which are restocked after 2 weeks. This room also consists of computer which the staff teachers use. Children choose books in their library period and return the books and choose new one in the next library period.

Basketball Court

Was established in 1992.

This place is mostly the most favourite place for the students of Lokhandwala Foundation School. Students, primary or secondary love this place and play many games in the games period. The area is large and two big baskets at two opposite sides are there. On the other side of our school basket ball court, there is a small park wich is controlled by the municipality corporation. Students play many games like:- Dodgeball, basketball, volleyball, Kho-Kho, etc.

Auditorium

Was established in 1992.

This place is where special performances and entertainments happen in school. I think it is my favorite place in the whole school. Special day like "International Yoga Day" happens. There is this secret of the auditoriom which is very exiting. the auditorium screen can turn 180°. That means it can turn to the entrance of the school! Many more special days like:- Christmas Eve, School Annual Day,Teachers Day, Childrens Day,Independance Day, Republic Day, etc are celebrated in our school by performing on the auditorium.
